Good to Know

QUESTIONS

What areas do you serve?

I'm based on Black Mountain and serve the surrounding Kelowna area — Black Mountain, Kettle Valley, Joe Rich, and Rutland. Not sure if you're in range? Just ask.

How do I get a quote?

Call me at 780-972-4848 or fill out the form below. I'll come walk your property in person — usually within the week — and give you a clear, no-obligation quote.

Do you offer one-time jobs or just regular service?

Both. I do weekly and bi-weekly lawn care, seasonal cleanups, and one-off jobs like pressure washing, hedge trimming, or repairs. Tell me what you need and we'll sort out what works.

Are you insured?

Yes — fully insured. Your property is treated with the same care I'd give my own.

Do you handle snow removal in winter?

I do. Driveway shoveling, walkway and step clearing, and ice control. Black Mountain winters are no joke — I'll keep you clear and safe. Seasonal contracts are available.

Can you look after my property while I'm away?

Absolutely — this is a big part of what I do. If you're a snowbird or away for work, I keep up your regular maintenance and snow clearing and send you a quick photo or update so you always know how things look. You come home to a property that never skipped a beat.

Do you take on strata or rental properties?

Yes. I take on smaller multi-unit stratas and complexes, plus rental and investment properties, where hands-on personal attention matters more than a big crew. You get one point of contact, consistent service, simple invoicing, and proof of insurance and references on request.
